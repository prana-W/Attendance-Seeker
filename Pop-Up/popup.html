Attendance Seeker

Attendance Seeker is a powerful browser extension designed for students. It helps you check attendance, calculate skippable classes, and find required classes to reach your target attendance.

Features and How to Use

🔹 Check Another Student's Attendance:
- Select the student from the dropdown.
- Wait for 5 seconds for the update.

🔹 Find Skippable Classes:
- Enter your target attendance in the Maximum Attendance Limit.
- Press Enter, and the site will update with the total skippable classes in each subject.

🔹 Find Required Classes to Cross a Target:
- Click the Switch button.
- Enter the desired attendance limit.
- The site will update with the required classes in each subject.

Stay in control of your attendance and plan smartly!
